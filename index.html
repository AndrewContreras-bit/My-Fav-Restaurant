Cheddars

And why it's my favorite

Cheddar's Scratch Kitchen is my favorite restaurant because it has amazing food and cheap prices. Founded in 1979, the restaurant chain has grown to over 170 locations across 28 states. The chain's menu features a variety of items, including appetizers, soups, salads, main courses, combos, comfort food, side dishes, kids meals, desserts, and beverages. The location is near a nice view with little wait time.
